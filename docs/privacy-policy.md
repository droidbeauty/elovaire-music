# Elovaire Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 23, 2026
**Last updated:** July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elovaire (package name
`elovaire.music.droidbeauty.app`) handles information. Elovaire is provided by
**Droid Beauty**.

Elovaire is an offline music player. It does not require an account and does
not include advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, remote lyrics, remote
artwork, remote metadata matching, or an app-operated cloud service.

## Information handled on your device

Elovaire may process the following information locally:

- audio files and folders you select, file names, content URIs, titles,
  artists, albums, genres, technical audio details, embedded tags, artwork,
  and embedded or sidecar lyrics;
- playlists, favorites, smart playlists, playback queue and position,
  playback history, play counts, recent items, and search history;
- app, playback, equalizer, language, theme, and layout settings;
- metadata, artwork, and lyrics you manually choose to write to supported
  audio files;
- Android version, audio routes, decoder capabilities, and USB audio
  capabilities needed for playback; and
- bounded local process-exit and recovery diagnostics.

This information is stored in app-private files, preferences, and a local
database. Elovaire does not upload music, metadata, artwork, lyrics,
fingerprints, file paths, playlists, history, settings, or diagnostics.

## Local artwork, lyrics, and editing

Elovaire reads artwork and lyrics embedded in local audio files and local
sidecar LRC files. Artwork selected through Android's system picker and lyrics
entered by the user are processed locally. File edits occur only after a user
action and any write approval required by Android.

Elovaire does not download album covers, artist photographs, full lyrics, or
music metadata from online providers.

## External links

The About and Privacy screens contain links to websites such as Droid Beauty
profiles, Google Play, Ko-fi, Android Developers, Lucide, and the project
repository. Elovaire opens a link only after you choose it. Your browser and
the destination website then handle information under their own privacy
policies.

## Permissions and storage access

Elovaire may use:

- media or legacy storage read access, depending on Android version, to find
  and play audio;
- Storage Access Framework access to folders you explicitly select;
- file-specific write approval for a metadata, artwork, or lyrics edit;
- audio-setting and media-playback foreground-service access for playback; and
- optional USB host access for compatible USB audio devices.

Elovaire does not request Internet, all-files, photo/video-library, location,
contacts, phone, advertising ID, unknown-app installation, or package
installation permission.

## Backups

Android backup or device transfer may include a limited set of portable
Elovaire settings. Elovaire excludes music files, user collections, playback
runtime state, search and playback history, media identifiers, folder grants,
caches, diagnostics, temporary files, and mutation journals.

## Retention and deletion

Local app data remains until you clear it through an available app control,
clear Elovaire's data in Android settings, or uninstall the app. Clearing app
data or uninstalling does not delete music files. Metadata, artwork, or lyrics
you intentionally wrote into a music file remain in that file.

## Sharing and sale

Droid Beauty does not sell personal information. Elovaire does not share app
data with advertisers, analytics companies, content providers, or an
app-operated backend. Android backup, when enabled by the device, is controlled
by the operating-system or device provider.

## Security

Elovaire uses Android app-private storage, scoped media and document access,
storage-specific write approval, bounded local caches, and transactional media
editing. No storage system can be guaranteed absolutely secure.

## Your choices

You can revoke media or selected-folder access in Android settings, clear
search history and other data using available controls, clear all private app
data, or uninstall Elovaire.

Because Droid Beauty does not receive app data on a server, it generally
cannot identify or retrieve information stored only on your device.

## Children's privacy

Elovaire does not require an account, display targeted advertising, or
knowingly solicit personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Elovaire's data practices or legal obligations
change. The current version remains available at this repository location.
